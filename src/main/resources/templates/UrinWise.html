Welcome to UrinWise

The FRDISI Foundation, established in 2016, is a key player in public-private partnerships in Morocco, particularly in the field of research and innovation. CONMEDIC GROUPE, a Moroccan industrial group specializing in the manufacture and distribution of medical devices, is committed to revolutionizing medical monitoring.

Our urinary flow monitoring project provides patients with easy access to their test results and histories. This innovative site also equips doctors with the necessary tools to effectively monitor their patients' health, including real-time graph visualization. By facilitating access to medical data, we aim to improve communication between patients and healthcare professionals, thereby promoting proactive and informed management of medical conditions.
